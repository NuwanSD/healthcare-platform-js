Making health care better, Together

Medicare Hospital Colombo is an ultra-modern multi-specialty 120 bedded corporate hospital located within the ‘Hospital Zone’ of Narahenpita, Colombo. We are committed to providing compassionate and high-quality medical care of international standards with a focus on value based healthcare i.e. providing the best clinical outcomes at optimum cost. We also provide the best clinical talent of the country and supplemented it with a highly skilled and competent nursing and technical team, robust support services, a modern infrastructure,the latest medical equipment and diagnostics, with cutting edge technology and a pleasing ambiance.

Vision

To be a leading healthcare provider, recognized for excellence in patient-centered care, innovation, and community well-being.

Mission

To deliver compassionate, high-quality care that enhances lives, prioritizing safety, innovation, and trust.

Story

Medicare Hospital has been a trusted name in healthcare since our establishment in 2020. Built on a foundation of compassion and innovation, we are committed to improving the health of our community by providing high-quality, patient-centered care.

Today, Medicare Hospital is a regional leader in medical excellence, known for both our advanced technology and our unwavering dedication to compassionate service.

With 300 beds, including 70 in our critical care department, our hospital offers a range of spacious, modern rooms designed for patient comfort. At Medicare, we are proud to work with some of the best consultants, specialists, and medical professionals, all of whom are dedicated to achieving outstanding clinical outcomes and providing the highest levels of patient satisfaction.

Our facilities are equipped with state-of-the-art technology, including a modern surgical suite with multiple operating theaters and advanced intensive care units (ICUs).

Medicare also offers a fully equipped radiology department, a comprehensive physiotherapy unit, and specialized catheterization labs.

With over 1,500 dedicated professionals including 300 specialist consultants, 20 resident consultants, and a strong team of senior medical officers, nurses, and support staff, Medicare Hospital is committed to delivering care with empathy and excellence.

As a testament to our quality, we have been recognized with the prestigious Joint Commission International (JCI) accreditation, reflecting our commitment to meeting global standards in healthcare.
